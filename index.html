Why Horoscope ?

Prior to delving into horoscope predictions, it's imperative to grasp the underlying principles of astrology. Astrology serves as a divinatory discipline that entails scrutinizing the behaviors of prominent celestial bodies, such as the sun and moon, alongside their interactions with other planets. These celestial movements are believed to exert either positive or negative influences on an individual's life or a specific geographical area, contingent upon their timing and positioning relative to one another.
